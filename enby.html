Hey, you are valid!

Non binary people are hella valid.

We have existed for a long time, even if you have never heard of us :3.

There are non binary people who prefer to present themselves as feminine and some which prefer to present themselves as masculine, and some where it changes from day to day.
However, you can't go by appearance alone when deciding what gender they are or how to address them.
